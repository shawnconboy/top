Description

This arroz con pollo is an easy chicken and seasoned rice dish that's comforting and delicious. Easy to adjust to use chicken thighs, or leftover chicken.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 green bellpepper chopped

Steps

  1. First, you need to season your chicken
  2. Second, you need to grill your chicken a bit
  3. Third, you need to mix your cooked chicken with your rice
